Flights from Los Cabos

The private jet experience on Aero

Elevate your experience traveling to and from Los Cabos, Mexico with Aero. Your trip begins at the Los Cabos International Airport (SJD) inside the PrimeSky Private Terminal where you’ll be greeted and checked in by an Aero Host. Expect a calm, efficient experience from the moment you step out of your vehicle to the moment you board our iconic black jet. With a seamless direct flight aboard Aero, you can minimize your travel time and maximize your vacation time.

The Aero Experience
What to Expect
01
Seamless Curbside Check-in
Upon arrival at PrimeSky Executive Private Terminal at the San José del Cabo International Airport, you can park your car—short term or long term self-parking is available—or simply wave goodbye to your driver. Our Aero Hosts are standing by, waiting to check you in for your flight just 20 minutes before take off. Your luggage will be promptly collected, tagged, and secured on the jet. Golf bags and other oversized luggage will be handled with the utmost care and safely stowed until landing.
Taking Off This Summer
02
Relax in Our Private Terminal
The Primesky Executive Private Terminal is modern and spacious, with comfortable seating, pleasant air conditioning, and a quiet atmosphere. It has a convenient snack bar where you can order a coffee, soda, cocktail, or refreshments before boarding the jet. It’s the perfect setting to catch up on emails or lounge beside your loved ones.
Taking Off This Summer
03
Quick Boarding Process
The Aero Host Team will escort you on a short stroll across the tarmac to our sleek black jet. Say a quick hello to your pilot waiting planeside and snap a photo on our iconic colored stairway. Once aboard, you’ll be greeted by our friendly Flight Attendants, who will direct you to your seat. There’s plenty of space to store your personal belongings under the seat in front of you, and if you’ve brought a four-legged friend, you’ll have time to settle them in before take off.
Taking Off This Summer
04
In-Flight Travel at Its Finest
The Aero in-flight experience is designed with comfort and style in mind—each of the 16 oversized Italian leather seats features a deep recline and spacious leg room, ensuring maximum personal space. Marvel at our custom interiors, complete with state-of-the-art lighting and a custom sound system. Once the flight is airborne, complimentary dining, refreshments, and amenities await. Start with the premium open bar featuring both alcoholic and zero-proof selections, then taste organic dishes with fresh ingredients from Erewhon on flights to Los Angeles. Our Starlink Wi-Fi offers ultra-fast connectivity to stream and browse from the moment you step on board.
Taking Off This Summer
05
Arrive With Ease
After the jet touches down, we’ll have you on your way in record time. All luggage and sports equipment are returned within minutes of landing, and our dedicated passport agent guides you through customs quickly and efficiently when traveling internationally. You’ll avoid crowded aisles, long waits at baggage claim, and the traffic that inevitably follows departing a major commercial airport.

FAQs

Aero operates from the Primesky Premium Private FBO at Los Cabos International Airport located at Ctra. Transpeninsular Km. 43.5 S/N, San José del Cabo, B. C. S, México.

Guests must provide their passport information at least 12 hours before departure. Sign into your Aero account and use your phone’s camera to scan your passport, or simply upload a saved image. If visas or other documents are required for your travels, please send them to passports@aero.com.

Guests must check in 20 minutes prior to the scheduled departure time for all Aero flights. We are unable to check in guests after this time.

Yes, complimentary short-term and long-term parking is available.

Private Airport Transfers: Transcabo is Los Cabos’ premier car service, offering airport transfers to resorts and private residences along the coastline. Contact our 24/7 Concierge to assist you with reservations.

Car Rentals: National Car Rental is located inside the FBO, and features a modern fleet of sedans, SUVs, and vans.

Rideshare Services & Taxis: Requesting an Uber to or from the FBO is easy. Taxis are available upon request; please contact contact our Concierge for more information.

02
The best places to eat and drink in Los Cabos
Nestled in the foothills of the Sierra de la Laguna Mountains, Flora Farms is a 25-acre organic working farm offering tours and cooking classes for groups of all sizes. The onsite restaurant, Flora’s Field Kitchen, serves farm-to-table dishes using ingredients grown on the property or sourced locally. You will find everything from house-made sausages and homemade seasonal jams to the popular veggie-laden beet Bloody Marys and mango salsa on the menu. Meanwhile, the lovely restaurant setting—a modern farmhouse surrounded by lush gardens—looks like it was lifted straight out of a fairytale.

The best things to do in Los Cabos
